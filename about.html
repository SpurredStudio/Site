About Spurred Studio

Spurred Studio is a small development studio with the hopes of creating memorable and enjoyable experiences for our users. Most of the content on our site is very rough around the edges and will need time to mature into their full potential.

Business Model

Our business model is to produce free content for those looking to learn about programming or engineering. Apps that we produce will include nonaggression advertisements which would not inhibit the users ability to use the app. These apps would also include a premium version that would remove the ads, but at no point would features be locked to specific versions unless resources demand it. Games produced by us would use a similar business model but the type of game would control exactly how its business model is designed.
